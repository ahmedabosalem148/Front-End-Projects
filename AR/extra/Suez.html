SUEZ CANAL TRANSIT

We offer Remarkable Agency services supported by a team of highly experienced professionals to meet the specific needs of every vessel at any port call.

Our Services can be tailored to your needs
Services include:

  • Registration at the Canal Authorities for First-time transit.
  • Settlement of estimated/actual cost differentials within 72h of transit
  • Advice on all documentation required by the Suez Canal Authority.
  • Preparation of priority bookings
  • Smooth transit without delay.
  • Complete record on transit.
  • Maintenance requirements.
