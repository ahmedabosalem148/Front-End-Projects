PORT AGENCY IN ALLEGYPTIAN PORTS

An extra hour in port can cost thousands of dollars. Our port agency services are based on years of experience to handle all types of vessels, a high level of competence, and standardized service concepts that are adapted to local and customer requirements. Saratoga shipping agency is so keen to render our excellent relations with port authorities for fast & efficient vessel turn around The range of services provided by our professional staff includes the following services.

  • Prearrival arrangements
  • Crew Matters (Joining / Repatriation / Visa/ Medical)
  • Shore Excursions
  • Cash to Master
  • Ships spare parts delivery
  • Ships Stores & Provision
  • Ships repairs
  • Supplying freshwater
  • Bunkering coordination
  • Coordination for Marine surveyors
  • Notes of protests (Notarization)
